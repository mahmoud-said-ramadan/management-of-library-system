Library Rules:-
 1-Library open From 9 Am:7 Pm
 2- All documents that are taken out of the Library are to be registered as a loan.
 3- The borrower is personally responsible for everything registered as a loan on his/her library card.
 4- Documents that are borrowed from the Library cannot be loaned to a third party.
 5- Loans are to be returned by the expiry date of the loan period.
 6- Making notes in documents is considered as damaging them.
 7- Documents that are lost or damaged must be replaced in accordance with the Library’s regulations and charges.
